-Connecting Multiple Sensors Solution!!!
https://arduino.stackexchange.com/questions/67553/changing-i2c-address-of-mpu-6050
- if you connect AD0 to ground, the I2C address is 0b1101000 (binary) or 0x68 (hexadecimal)
- if you connect AD0 to Vcc, the I2C address is 0b1101001 (binary) or 0x69 (hexadecimal)
